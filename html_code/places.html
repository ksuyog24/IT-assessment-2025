Places I Visited in Nepal

  • Jarsingpauwa – this is my fav place where i visit and there i found beautiful hill area with peaceful views.
  • Dodhara Chandani – Its homes town too and it is famous suspension bridge and riverside beauty.
  • Kalinchowk – This is my second most fav place and there i found Snowy mountains and temple on top!
  • Bajura – It is a Remote village with untouched natural beauty.
  • Bardiya National Park – This is my first national park that i visit and i found many wildlife , green jungle safari experience and a beautiiful river.
  • Ghandruk – Traditional Gurung village with Annapurna view.
  • Butwal – this is energetic and noisy city with culture and hills.
  • Kathmandu – Our capital city full of heritage and beautiful views.
